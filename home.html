Our Story

Crafting Culinary Excellence

Originating in Yoshino, Kansai—a place where cherry blossoms paint the landscape—Sakura Delights captures the serenity and beauty that inspired its founders, chefs Hiroshi and Yuki. Our mission is simple: to offer an oasis of peace where every dish celebrates authentic flavours and each moment is touched by caring service.

With roots deep in Japan's cherry blossom heartland, we've expanded our peaceful haven all the way to Singapore, delivering the same commitment to quality, freshness, and sustainable dining. Here, each meal is more than just food—it's a tranquil experience.
